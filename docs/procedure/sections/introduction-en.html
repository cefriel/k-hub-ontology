Introduction back to ToC

The Procedure module of the Knowledge Hub Ontology was developed to support the retrieval of information from procedures described in documents such as service manuals, user manuals etc... used in the manufacturing field. The Procedure module models the procedures (also called Plan) for performing maintenance on manufacturing products or services. The Plan is decomposed in Steps which needs to be followed in the right order. Sometimes a Step is represented by an entire procedure as well.
